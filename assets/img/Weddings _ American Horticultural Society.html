    • Weddings

    Congratulations on your upcoming nuptials! River Farm offers intimate settings with spacious lawns facing beautiful gardens, where you can exchange your vows and celebrate with friends and family.

    A River Farm Wedding

    With 25 acres of lawn, meadows, woodland, formal gardens, and scenic views of the Potomac, River Farm is a place of natural year-round beauty and a perfect showcase for your wedding any time of year. Review the photos below to see not only the event spaces available but also the splendor that will surround your wedding. Please call (703) 768-5700 ext. 114 or email us at rentals@ahs.org to schedule a tour. We're looking forward to meeting you!

    The River Farm Property

    Your rental doesn't just include our beautiful gardens, grounds, and spacious patio -- you also get dancing in the ballroom and mingling areas on the main level of the Estate House, as well as a dressing room on the second level. Please look at the pictures below to get an idea of the facilities available.

    Ceremony and Reception

    River Farm offers endless possibilities when choosing the perfect location for your wedding vows and reception. You might choose our the meadow and the Potomac River as your ceremony backdrop, or perhaps you'll want to say your vows in the formal gardens. No matter where you choose, you and your visitors will have an unforgettable day here. The pictures below will give you an idea of what your day will be like. By arranging a tour of the House and gardens you'll experience firsthand the magic of a River Farm wedding. Visit our Flickr gallery for additional River Farm event photos and please call (703) 768-5700 ext. 114 or email rentals@ahs.org to arrange your tour.
